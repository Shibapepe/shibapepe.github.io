What is ShibaPepeAI

A community meme & NFT token

ShibaPepeAI is a unique meme token that is built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). As a community-driven token, That enables users to take part in the decision-making process through their opinions, which we aims to implement in our executive decisions.
One of the key features of ShibaPepeAI its staking and farming capabilities, which allows users to earn additional rewards by locking up their tokens for a certain period of time. Additionally, ShibaPepeAI offers NFTs and a trading robot, further expanding its utility and potential benefits for users.
To help maintain its stability, ShibaPepeAI has limited its token supply to a minimal level, and also plans to burn tokens regularly. This strategy is designed to help keep the token's price stable and prevent inflation, making it a potentially attractive investment option for those looking for a meme token with real utility.

Features

Burning

ShibaPepeAI having a normal supply of a meme token, this supply will be burnt out over time to stabilize the price and also to regulate the amount of the token in circulation.

Buybacks

This will be done over time, the revenue generated by the project will be used to buy back some of the supply which will be burnt.

Staking

ShibaPepeAI Token allows holders to stake their tokens and earn additional rewards while supporting the operations of the network. Staking provides a secure and decentralized way to support the network without the need for expensive hardware or technical expertise.

Anti-whale system

An Anti-whale system will be adopted to prevent users from buying more than a specific amount at a single transaction and also selling off a particular amount at a single transaction

Max buy/sells

A max buy & sell system will be implemented to prevent high volatility in price, this will help regulate price movement for the community members.
